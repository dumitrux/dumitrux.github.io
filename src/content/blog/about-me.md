## My Approach

I like to follow a systematic/engineering approach in my work: first understand the problem, then find the right balance between all the factors. I try to keep a humble mindset and always ask why.

## Career Background

Most of my experience is in healthcare and pharma. I have worked on improving applications for patient enrollment, building multitenant platforms for national health services, and supporting data platforms during COVID-19. Certified in multiple Microsoft technologies (Solutions Architect, AI Engineer, Fabric, Security, among others), GitHub, and infrastructure tools like CKA and Terraform.
